Shipping & Returns

Shipping

Free shipping on all orders over $50.

  • Orders are processed within 24 hours (business days)
  • Standard shipping: 3-5 business days
  • Expedited shipping: 1-2 business days ($9.99)
  • International shipping available to select countries

Returns

30-day satisfaction guarantee.

  • Unopened products can be returned within 30 days
  • Opened products: contact us if you're not satisfied
  • Return shipping covered by Solvra for defective items
  • Refunds processed within 5-7 business days

Damaged Orders

If your order arrives damaged, please:

  1. Take photos of the damage within 48 hours
  2. Email us at hello@solvra.com with your order number
  3. We'll send a replacement immediately, no questions asked
