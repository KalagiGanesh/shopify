About Solvra

Our mission is to fuel human potential through functional coffee.

Our Story

Solvra was born from a simple observation: high-performers deserve better coffee. We saw too many professionals relying on sugary energy drinks or cheap coffee that left them jittery and crashed.

We set out to create functional coffee blends that combine premium beans with scientifically-backed nootropics and adaptogens. The result? Clean, sustained energy that supports deep focus without the crash.

Our Ingredients

Every Solvra product is crafted with:

  • Premium Arabica Beans: Sourced from sustainable farms in Colombia and Ethiopia
  • L-Theanine: An amino acid found in green tea that promotes calm alertness
  • Lion's Mane Mushroom: Supports cognitive function and mental clarity
  • Ashwagandha: An adaptogen that helps manage stress and promotes balance

Sustainability

We believe great coffee shouldn't cost the earth. That's why we:

  • Source from Rainforest Alliance certified farms
  • Use carbon-neutral shipping methods
  • Packaging is 100% recyclable and compostable
  • Donate 1% of profits to environmental causes

Our Promise

Every product comes with our 30-day Focus Guarantee. If you don't feel a noticeable difference in your mental clarity and focus after 14 days of consistent use, we'll give you a full refund. No questions asked.
